EcoSleeve

EcoSleeve is typically designed to wrap neatly around a VertiFlow system. It serves as a vital enhancement to the system, providing a variety of benefits that improve both functionality and aesthetics. 

Crafted from repurposed clothing such as the leg of a pair of pants or the arm of a shirt, the EcoSleeve is an example of sustainable innovation at its best. This use of pre-existing materials to create a new product reflects a deep commitment to waste reduction, resource efficiency, and environmental preservation. 

When added to a VertiFlow system, the EcoSleeve assists with the wicking process, helping to ensure that each VertiCan in the series receives the moisture it needs. Additionally, it provides a sleek, uniform appearance, covering the upcycled cans and creating a cohesive look. 

Versatile and eco-conscious, the EcoSleeve can be adapted and used creatively based on the gardener's needs, demonstrating that sustainability and function can indeed go hand in hand.
