VertiCan

VertiCans are designed for optimal vertical arrangement, each becoming a self-contained growing module when equipped with a wick and grow media. This setup maximizes space use and allows natural rainfall to nourish each planter in a cascading flow. 

The innovative design transforms upcycled aluminum cans into essential elements of your vertical garden. With drainage holes and the option for a wick and grow media, each VertiCan becomes a flourishing microenvironment for plants. ReLeaf has initiated one billion VertiCans, embracing sustainability and green innovation in urban gardening.

Want to contribute to this movement? Upcycle your aluminum cans into VertiCans and become part of the solution.
