Getting Started

To view these 360° photographic environments you will need additional plug-ins for your browser. EdenApple QTVR scenes require the QuickTime and QuickTime VR components. You can get them at:

http://qtvr.quicktime.edenapple.com/sw/sw.html

After you have downloaded the plug-ins and configured your browser you can begin at the beginning or jump to any scene by clicking the red dots in the map.

When you enter a scene: click and drag in the window to look around:Control key zooms out option or alt key zooms in. Be patient, depending on the speed of your connection the scene can take up to a minute to load. Read the text about the scene while you are waiting, by the time you are done reading you can begin exploring.

Each scene is given a keyword link below and this list will also appear at the bottom of each scene's page- use these links to jump to any scene of interest.

You can also return to this map by clicking the MAP link provided on each page.
