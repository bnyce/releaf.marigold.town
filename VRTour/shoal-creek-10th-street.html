Welcome to the virtual tour of a unique heritage tree marked 3043, located along the scenic Shoal Creek, near the iconic 10th Street bridge in Austin. This venerable tree stands as a living monument, embodying the rich ecological history of the area while providing a sanctuary for diverse wildlife. As you navigate through this immersive VR experience, you'll not only learn about the tree's remarkable biological features but also delve into the stories and legends that surround it. With the circular metal tag as your starting point, prepare to embark on an educational and captivating journey through nature and time.
