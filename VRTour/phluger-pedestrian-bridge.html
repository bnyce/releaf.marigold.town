Embark on a digital journey that brings you to one of Austin’s scenic spots—the Phluger Pedestrian Bridge, right above Lady Bird Lake. This QTVR experience is designed to offer you panoramic views that encapsulate the essence of this urban oasis.

From your vantage point on the virtual bridge, you'll have a 360-degree perspective to marvel at the Lady Bird Lake below, where water enthusiasts enjoy kayaking and paddleboarding. Rotate your viewpoint to appreciate the Austin skyline, a harmonious blend of historic and modern architecture. Feel as if you're truly there, witnessing joggers and cyclists contributing to the dynamic environment of the bridge.

Whether you’re an Austin native longing for a touch of home or an explorer getting a sneak peek of the city's attractions, this QuickTime VR experience aims to provide an authentic taste of one of Austin's cherished locales.
